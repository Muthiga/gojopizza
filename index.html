How to place your order.

  • Select specifications of your choice from pizza size, crust and toppings.
  • Leave any comments on special diets or demands you might have for your order. eg("no onions, no olvies etc")
  • Enter the number of pizzas you would like with that the same specifications.
  • Press order to submit your first order.
  • If you want to order another pizza with different specifications, re-enter new specifications in the blank input areas.
  • Press order again.
  • Repeat the steps for multiple and different orders.
  • After submitting your orders, select your choice to get the pizzas, either delivered or reserve a table but due to Covid-19 protocols we are only serving in-shop take away and delivery orders only.

    • For Deliveries:
    1. After placing your order, you will be notified of an extra charge for the delivery, press ok to continue
    2. Select the area of delivery
    3. Enter the correct address you want the Pizza to be delivered at. Note (Please counter-check your address to avoid late or misplaced orders.)
    4. Submit your information.
  • For Reservations:

    Dear customer, due to the Covid-19 protocols and regulations, we are currently not offering any reservation or in-shop dining. We are sorry for this inconvinience and we will update you as soon as anything changes.

  • Press the checkout button to submit all your final orders and see the summarry of your purchases and the total cost you will pay.
